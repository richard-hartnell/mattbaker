Museum of Curious Things

The Museum of Curious Things is a mobile curiosity museum filled with oddities from around the world. One of the most unique attractions available in the Northwest United States, our museum leaves audiences intrigued, laughing, and talking about what they saw in our museum for days afterward. Our unique collection is loaded with cultural artifacts, unusual taxidermy, crypto-zoological samples, anatomical oddities and historical mysteries. We strive to stimulate the brain’s natural curiosity and to educate people of all ages on the wonders of the unknown with the intent to preserve a part of American culture that has been lost. The Museum is housed in a 27ʹ vintage Airstream Trailer and was quoted by Seattle Magazine as “the coolest thing at the event.”
