David DaVinci

The Daniel DaVinci Comedy Circus Show is chock full of original stunts, audience participation, and an endless stream of hilarious comedy patter. Daniel DaVinci has set two Guinness records, made three appearances on "The Tonight Show," performed for The President of the United States, and toured with comedians Billy Crystal and Robin Williams.

Versatile and easy to work with, all of his appearances are family-friendly, and guaranteed to be the hit of your event. With over 25 years of experience, there hasn't been a crowd yet that hasn't fallen in love with his sense of humor, and unique spin on the art of juggling.
