Circus Luminescence

Circus Luminescence is an award-winning comedy juggling group from Portland, Oregon that specializes in energetic, contemporary vaudeville shows that include incredible partner juggling, breathtaking acrobatics, epic combination tricks, state-of-the-art L.E.D technology, and loads of laughter. Circus L is a scalable enterprise with entertainment options that feature artists performing hula-hooping, contortion, stilt-walking, go-go dancing, tarot reading, and more!
