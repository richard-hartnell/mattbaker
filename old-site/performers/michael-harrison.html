Michael Harrison

Michael Harrison is an extraordinarily talented ventriloquist, puppeteer and comedian. He has worked along side celebrity acts Kelly Clarkson, The Muppets and Boyz to Men. Michael has been a headline act on over 30 luxury cruise ships including 12 consecutive years and 2500 performances for Disney Cruise Line.

Michael took his talents to a new level when he appeared on the hugely successful television show, “America’s Got Talent”. He finished as one of the finalist and received glowing reviews from the celebrity judges.

These days you can find Michael performing across the country at numerous venues where his show is always in high demand - comedy clubs, performing arts centers, corporate shows, fairs and festivals.

“There are very few entertainers who can make you laugh until it hurts, Michael Harrison is one of them”.
