Susan Rice

For 30 years Susan Rice has been sharing her quirky sense of humor with audiences young and old. She is funny, familiar and that is probably the reason she’s still single. She is never a stranger. Rice is the kind of comic that brings everyone back to that one beloved relative who was the first to show up and the last to leave. Everyone loved her until the second glass of wine.

Raised in the Pacific Northwest Rice still resides in Portland but lived and worked in Los Angeles for over 15 years. She has performed all over the US and Canada and has been seen on Comedy Central, Showtime and was a featured performer at the HBO’s Comedy Festival at Caesar’s Palace in Las Vegas and Portland Oregon’s Bridgetown Comedy Festival and is now working with Relay for Life to help groups raise funds to fight cancer.

She has performed with some of the greats; Jerry Seinfeld, Paula Poundstone, Maria Bamford. She has done all the comedy shows of the 90’s A& E’s Evening at the Improv, Showtime’s Comedy on the Road All Stars.

When asked recently why she chose a career in Stand Up comedy Rice replied “It got to a point where making people laugh on the bus just got annoying.

With all her years in comedy Rice has maintained her love of the art.” It is such a privilege to make people laugh it crosses all boundaries of age and sex and gives everyone a moment of respite from their everyday troubles. It’s good for the soul.

When Susan hits the stage the audience automatically finds her fun and familiar. She is the perfect fit for any show or event.
