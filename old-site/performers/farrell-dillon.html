Farrell Dillon

Farrell Dillon is one of the top comedy magicians in the world. He has been seen performing for clients like; Coca-Cola, Disney, Harrah's Entertainment, Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines, MTV, FOX, Nickelodeon, Comedy Central and Pepsi. You may also recognize him from his 13 episode run on the hit television series Masters of Illusion. Farrell is currently the host of the international touring phenomenon, Masters of Illusion LIVE! Farrell has a unique style that creates a perfect blend of comedy, magic, skill, and illusion.
