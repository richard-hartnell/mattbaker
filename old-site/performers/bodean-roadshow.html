The Bodean Roadshow

Sparks will fly when Pete and Bree BoDean bring the BoDean Roadshow to town! Combine a southern hillbilly with the skills to juggle anything in the junkyard with a western cowgirl with the talent to wrangle anything on the ranch and you get a country comedy duo able to entertain any crowd! Flaming pitchforks, lariats, spinning hubcaps, whips, daredevil leaps into overalls, juggling with the feet, rope tricks, death defying towering rings of fire and more combined with a big dose of comedy and audience participation will leave the crowd astounded and entertained. The BoDean Roadshow travels anywhere and everywhere, and is unafraid of draughts or floods, fire or knives.
