Professor Scotty Walsh

Professor Scotty Walsh is sought worldwide for his abilities as a mind-reader, magician, circus artist, and physical comedian. He has performed across the United States, Italy, France, and was a recent headliner for the National Circus Festival of Ireland.

His family comedy magic show is more than a show, it's an event! It features lively audience participation, spaghetti juggling, magic, mind-reading, and a thrilling Houdini-like escape - all amid Professor Scotty Walsh's signature eccentricities.

Mr. Walsh holds Masters degrees in Physical Theatre as well as Strategic Public Policy and is currently a doctoral researcher at University College Cork in Ireland on the topic of theatrical mind reading and mind control.
