Here's how to add new artists to your site.

GoDaddy doesn't make this super easy but lk you through it.

The only hassle is adding an artist's image. Everything else is done in this spreadsheet:
Artist Spreadsheet

Add all the information you want about the new artist there.
Note that each new paragraph in their description should go into its own column.

To add the image, go to your sites' File Manager:

https://p3plzcpnl506885.prod.phx3.secureserver.net:2083/cpsess8794044850/frontend/jupiter/filemanager/index.html

Then enter the public_html folder:


Then enter the "mattbakercomedy.com" folder, then the "assets" folder, then the "images" folder after that:

Then click on the Upload button:


Then upload the image you want to use for the new artist, and add its filename (e.g. "curt-carlyle.jpg") to the spreadsheet in the "IMAGE NAME" column.

Then you're done! If you ever want to change an artist on the site, you can just change it on the spreadsheet; to remove an artist, you can just delete their row.
