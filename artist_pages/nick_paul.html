Nick Paul

Nick Paul is a Los Angeles-based magician and comedian known for his unique combination of physical comedy, magic, and quick wit. He has been seen on Conan, Netflix, and Penn and Teller Fool Us. And has consulted for The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon and Magic for Humans with Justin Willman.

Nick Paul is a regular featured performer at the world-famous Magic Castle in Hollywood and tours colleges, fortune 500 companies, cruise ships, and theaters. His show has been seen in over a dozen countries worldwide.

His Fringe Festival show Impossible Feats of Fake Magic received rave reviews. Awarded critics pick for "Best Variety Show" by the Orlando Sentinel and voted “Top 10 Shows to See” by Time Out LA.

Most notably, he recently won the award for “Greatest Magician….in his family.”
