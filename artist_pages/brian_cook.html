Brian Cook

Brian has been thrilling audiences around the world for the last 15 years with his unique style of magic. He is an award-winning magical entertainer, whose engaging style blends pure sleight of hand with comedy, transforming any venue into a theatrical experience.

He is the current Washington state Territory Vice President for the prestigious International Brotherhood of Magicians, the youngest to ever hold this position. Brian’s talents won Seattle’s magician of the year twice as well as the Pomeroy award for artistic excellence.

Brian has performed for world renowned companies like Microsoft, RedBull, Frito Lay and Staples. Experience for yourself why companies bring him back year after year to treat their clients and employees to a truly unforgettable memory.
