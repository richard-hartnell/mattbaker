Thom Wall

When Thom Wall walks on stage, you get high energy and smart comedy. With a habit of delighting audiences of all sizes, Thom seamlessly combines award-winning juggling, acrobatic stunts, and theatrical comedy into a show that will leave you smiling down to your toes.

Have you ever seen a grown man jump-rope with a string of sausages? Have you seen anyone dive through a bicycle rim that’s been lined with rat-traps? Thom’s stage spectacular is just that – spectacular! It’s a celebration of human absurdity, backed up with some of the most surprising and innovative juggling technique in the world today.

Thom can adapt his act to fit any venue – from casinos to trade events, rock festivals to theme parks, Thom isn’t just a world-class juggler, he’s a versatile performer who can make your next event shine!
