Nate Jester

Seattle-based Nate Jester has become one of the nation’s top illusionists. His perfectly balanced combination of magic and comedy has entertained audiences worldwide. You may have witnessed his skills on America’s Got Talent, Bravo’s LOLWork and Oprah’s The Life You Want Tour. It’s easy to see why Nate has accurately been dubbed, “The Ace of Illusions!”
