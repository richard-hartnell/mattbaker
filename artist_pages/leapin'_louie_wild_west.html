Leapin' Louie Wild West

David Lichtenstein has performed Leapin’ Louie shows in 31 different countries around the world. HIs amazing comedy cowboy trick show has The 75 foot big loop, whip cracking, trick roping hilarious audience participation, spoken and physical comedy and a whole lot of fun for everyone. He has performed at dozens of county and state fairs around the country, many of them over and over again. 2015 saw him performing 25 theater shows at the Edinburgh (Scotland) Fringe Festival, the largest theater festival in the world and receiving excellent reviews. In Spring of 2016, he has performed in Dubai, Kuwait (countries number 30 and 31), Arizona, California, Texas plus the usual local Oregon and Washington shows.
