Duane Goad

Duane Goad quickly became one of comedy’s rising young stars when he stepped onstage to compete, and ultimately win, a comedy contest at the legendary Improv Comedy Club. Since then, this in-demand comic has entertained comedy club and corporate audiences all over the US and Canada.

Besides headlining his own shows, Duane has had the opportunity to be the opening act for some of the top names in comedy such as Jim Gaffigan, Lewis Black, Margaret Cho, Carlos Mencia, and Dana Gould, just to name a few.

Duane has starred in numerous commercials for Microsoft, Nintendo, Bank of America, Ford, Molson Beer, and the FX Network. Other TV appearances include CTV, Comedy Central and E!, as well as the independent film, '80's Ending'.
