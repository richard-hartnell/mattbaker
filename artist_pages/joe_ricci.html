Joe Ricci

Joe Ricci is a dynamic juggler and performer from Seattle. Originally from NY, he moved to Seattle to blossom as a full time entertainer. He holds two Guinness World Records and has performed in over 12 countries. His show features hilarious comedy, amazing juggling, tearing a phone book in half, breakdancing and a variety of interactive routines that will keep your crowd engaged. Joe offers walk around performances, street style shows, workshops and full length stage productions.
